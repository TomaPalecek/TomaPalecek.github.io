About me

Hello, I'm Toma, a game designer.

As an engineering graduate who found my true calling in game design, I am passionate about creating immersive and engaging gameplay experiences that tell compelling stories. Game design is the perfect intersection of my technical background, creative interests, and lifelong love of gaming.

Collaboration is at the heart of my approach to game design. Working closely with different departments to find common ground and create solutions that work for everyone is something that I thrive on. Whether it's designing core gameplay mechanics, building immersive worlds, or writing engaging storylines, I bring a unique combination of technical and creative skills to the table. I am dedicated to creating games that challenge and inspire players while also bringing people together through the power of gaming.

April 2023

Badland

Badlands is a post-apocalyptic Role Playing First Person Shooter with survival elements. As a game designer for this project, my responsibilities included developing the game's storylines and quest designs, creating unique and dynamic characters and situations, and designing weapons with distinct mechanics and a distinct look and feel.

February 2023

Ancestor Incarnate

As a game designer for Ancestor Incarnate, my primary responsibilities included building the game world and designing the core gameplay mechanics, controls, and loop. The game is a unique and immersive experience that explores the concept of life after death, where the player must upload their soul to the Ancestral Cloud before their physical body fails.
